Ahmad Dahlan Integrated System and Technology Version 2

The Problem

The Ahmad Dahlan University (UAD) is a university that employs an academic system in its teaching and learning processes. However, the academic system used is currently only available in web format.

The university aims to enhance its academic system by developing a mobile application. In addition to keeping up with the times, UAD seeks to provide a better experience for both students and faculty members in their teaching and learning activities through the use of a mobile application for the academic system.

Design Process

The initial step involves engaging in discussions with stakeholders to explore the reasons, goals, and expectations. This is followed by gathering as much data as possible from the ultimate users (students and faculty members) and then sorting through the data to make informed decisions.

The process then proceeds to the design phase, which includes creating a Sitemap, Sketch, Wireframing, High-fidelity, Design System, Prototyping, and finally conducting testing with the ultimate users. Importantly, this design process is iterative, involving repeated cycles.
