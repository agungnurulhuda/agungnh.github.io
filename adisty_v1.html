The Problem

The Ahmad Dahlan University (UAD) is a university that employs an academic system in its teaching and learning processes. However, the academic system used is currently only available in web format.

The university aims to enhance its academic system by developing a mobile application. In addition to keeping up with the times, UAD seeks to provide a better experience for both students and faculty members in their teaching and learning activities through the use of a mobile application for the academic system.

The application to be designed will be used by students, lecturers, and staff of Ahmad Dahlan University. Therefore, there will be three applications to be developed, each with a different interface tailored to the specific needs of the users.

Design Process

The initial step involves engaging in discussions with stakeholders to explore the reasons, goals, and expectations. This is followed by gathering as much data as possible from the ultimate users (students and faculty members) and then sorting through the data to make informed decisions.

The process then proceeds to the design phase, which includes creating a Sitemap, Sketch, Wireframing, High-fidelity, Design System, Prototyping, and finally conducting testing with the ultimate users. Importantly, this design process is iterative, involving repeated cycles.

Develop Process

After presenting the UI/UX design results and finalizing the design, the next step is to proceed with the application development using the Flutter framework. My skills in Flutter enable me to directly collaborate with the development team from Ahmad Dahlan University.

In the development process, my role involves being responsible for slicing or translating the design into the actual application form to ensure it looks identical or precise to the design.

Launching

It is a joy of its own when the UI/UX design that I have developed can be implemented through the development stage and be utilized by everyone.

The application launch was featured on the official YouTube account of Ahmad Dahlan University and was conducted by the university's rector on December 19, 2023. The application is set to be available for use in January 2024.
