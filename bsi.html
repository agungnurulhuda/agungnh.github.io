What's this

The Information Systems Bureau (BSI) is an implementing unit responsible for managing IT infrastructure, developing applications, website development, information security, technical support, and IT project management.

The BSI is planning a redesign of its official website, and I am actively involved in the website design.

Role & Design

I play a role as a UI/UX designer in the redesign process of the BSI website. The initial design phase involves conducting usability testing on the existing website to gather feedback on information accessibility and layout improvements.

The subsequent design stages include sketching, creating wireframes, developing high-fidelity designs, and crafting prototypes. The final step entails testing the new design and comparing it with the old design to assess the improvements.
